Patrik Malmquist is a tech loving geek that left development for strategy, project management and software development organization. In his role as Enterprise Mobility Manager for Sigma he works with architecture and solution selling within mobile and web solutions. Between 2005 and 2011 he was the software development manager for www.minpension.se and project manager for pension prognosis tools for web and mobile solutions for the Swedish Pensions Agency. You can follow him at his personal blog http://www.applitude.se that focuses on leading productive software development teams and on his Sigma blog that focuses on the mobile work life.
