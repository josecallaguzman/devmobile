Johan Sanneblad's presentation:
Game Development with Unity for Mobile Devices

Johan Sanneblad works as a consultant manager at HiQ. Johan has a Ph.D in mobile IT where he designed and implemented advanced cross-platform game development tools for companies such as Nokia and Sony Online Entertainment. Johan has worked at HiQ since 2005 in various roles including both developer and project leader, for clients such as Ericsson, HP, SJ, Skype, Sony Ericsson, Telia, Volvo Cars, Volvo Trucks, and many others. Johan has a genuine game interest, and created the game “Tomten” in Unity together with his wife Helena in his spare time during autumn 2011. The game was launched on December 13 and reached #13 in most downloaded games in the Swedish App Store in December.
